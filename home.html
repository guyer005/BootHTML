Weathering Latitudes: Investigation of Latitude by Weather

The goal of this project was to demonstrate the compelling relationship between latitude and weather.

The OpenWeather API was the data source for all of the 500 cities studied in this project.


In the end we sought to evaluate the strength and direction of the relationship between four different weather indicators and latitude.

What was found and what conclusions were reached? Please explore this website to see for yourself and learn about these exciting relationships!
