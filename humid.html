Latitude by Humidity

This image depicts the relationship between latitude and humidity (as %).

First of all we can see that there was a clear and unrealistic outlier in these data - as humidity % is capped at 100. Without that case we still see that there was little in the way of a correlation between the two variables. Rather the variance of the data increased as we moved away from the equator and fell as we approached the poles.
