Latitude by Temperature

This image depicts the relationship between latitude and temperature in Fahrenheit. The Pearson linear correlation between temperature and latitude was near zero (.003). However, that does not tell the full story as there is a quadratic trend due to our approaching and leaving the equator. If we split the plot into two parts around the 0 latitude mark, average temperature rises as we approach the equator and declines as we depart for the North pole.

Take Home Message: Getting closer to the equator tends to result in an increase in temperature!
